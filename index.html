Questions

How Do I Get Paid?

We send out payments every Monday. This will only include transactions that have been cleared. If a transaction has not yet been cleared by a card provider, it will have to wait until the next week.

When Do My Customers Get Billed?

We will start the billing process two days after the scheduled event. This gives you ample amount of time to let us know if there was a change in plan.

What if I Don't Want to Charge for an Appointment?

Not a problem. You'll be able to cancel individual or recurring scheduled payments with a click of a button.

Can I Modify the Recurring Payment Amount?

Sure thing. We'll just need to have your customer agree to the change before they are billed to make sure you are both on the same page.

How Will my Customer's Get Notified of the Charge?

After we charge a card, we'll send you and your customer an email letting them know the details of the transaction.

Does Yeti Only Work With Recurring Payments?

At the initial release, we will only cover recurring payments. If there is enough demand for it, we will build individual payments later down the road.

What About American Express and International Cards?

Not a problem for our pet Yeti. Bring 'em on!

Is there a fee for charge backs?

Yes, if your customer contacts their card provider and tell them your charge is bogus, you will charged $20 per charge back. This is why it is important for all of us to be on the same page.

What About Refunds?

If your customer asks for a refund, we'll send them the money back. No additional charge.

Do I get Charged for a Declined Payment?

Nope. Yeti doesn't roll that way. We'll even email your customer telling them their card was denied and ask them to provide a different card.

How Secure is This?

At Yeti Billing, we take security very seriously. The technolgy to process billing has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor, and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. This is the most stringent level of certification available. In addition, we use SSL to encrypt each request and verify that your customers are sending their financial information to the right place.
